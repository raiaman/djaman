About

Dj Aman has always had a passion for playing music that makes you want to move. His journey started back in 2024 as the dj of some event Bla Bla at Bla Bla place.

A proponent of all styles of latin music, Aman is known to incorporate a variety of genres whether it be breakbeat, house or techno.

Most recently, Aman has relocated to Paris, where he continues to do what he loves most, bring music to the people.
